Why Recruit @JNU

Jawaharlal Nehru University, established in 1969, is a vibrant campus. The university has an area of about 1000 acres and currently 7300 students are residing in 17 hostels and studying under around 550 reputed faculties. JNU is a university which believes in creating new heights in every field of education and in nurturing social awareness in society. JNU has 10 different schools including 4 special centres to provide quality education and social awareness. The School of Language, Literature and Cultural Studies (SLL&CS) is responsible for providing education not only in the language but also in the cultural and social aspects of other societies/countries.

More Reason

" Jawaharlal Nehru University is well-known in various fields for its research and its socially inclusive nature. A wide range of students from ten different schools including four special centers are capable to improve the working conditions of any institution. The Jawaharlal Nehru University Placement Cell (JNUPC) was opened in the year 2008, and we are happy to inform that since then we have been securing our students’ future. ".

Student

There are more than 8000 students enrolled in different courses in JNU. There are currently 50% MPhil/PhD students, 28% postgraduate and 16% graduate students studying in JNU. Engineering course has recently started with 1% of total students.
